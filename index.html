About me

An Electrical Engineer with a deep passion for automotive technology and innovation. With one year of hands-on experience in the automotive industry, I have enhanced my expertise in this field, specializing in model-based system design with a particular focus on HIL (Hardware-in-the-Loop) and MIL (Model-in-the-Loop) validation. These competencies are crucial for ensuring the performance, reliability, and robustness of automotive systems. Furthermore, I have a strong understanding of key automotive concepts such as Powertrain, HVAC, ADAS, AEB, and ABS systems. In my role, I played a key part in developing and automating test cases for the power steering control module (PSCM) for Volvo Car Corporation. This experience gave me valuable insights into functionality testing within the Vector HIL environment.
